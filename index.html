The Problem We're Solving

UK trade professionals and businesses have no reliable way to assess customer risk before accepting work.

For Construction

Construction firms are owed £22.6 billion in late payments, with the average firm waiting 107 days for payment.

71% of tradespeople have been victims of non-payment, with an average annual loss of £2,472 per tradesperson.

Source: Federation of Master Builders, Build UK

For Vehicle Traders

Vehicle-related complaints increased by 17% in 2022, with customer disputes costing dealers an average of £1,890 per case.

37% of vehicle dealers report that better customer information would have prevented costly disputes.

Source: Motor Ombudsman, NFDA

For Suppliers

Commercial suppliers wait an average of 60 days for payment, with 74% reporting challenges with specific problematic customers.

Small UK wholesalers write off an average of £10,000 per year in bad debts due to inadequate customer information.

Source: CICM UK, Department for Business and Trade
